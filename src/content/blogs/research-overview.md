Zhang Lab is organized around a clinical-translational loop: identify unmet needs in cardiovascular care, model disease mechanisms with experimental and computational platforms, and return validated insights to clinical practice.

Core directions include coronary intervention, vascular repair and regeneration, vascular organoids and organoid chips, and AI-enabled cardiovascular active health.
