We build and test wearable device systems for long-term cardiovascular monitoring outside the clinic. The work spans sensor hardware, physiological signal processing, and methods for integrating continuous data with clinical records. The goal is to understand whether real-world physiological trajectories can improve risk detection and inform preventive care.

- **Continuous monitoring**: Non-invasive wearable technologies for cardiac and vascular signal acquisition
- **Signal processing**: Methods for multi-modal physiological data analysis
- **Data integration**: Linking wearable data with clinical phenotypes and outcomes
- **Validation studies**: Assessing device performance and clinical utility in real-world settings
