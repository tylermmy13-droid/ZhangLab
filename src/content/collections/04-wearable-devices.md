## Research Focus

We develop and validate wearable device technologies for continuous cardiovascular monitoring and active health management, bridging medical science with engineering innovation.

### Key Areas:
- **Continuous cardiovascular monitoring**: Non-invasive wearable technologies for real-time cardiac monitoring
- **Physiological signal processing**: AI-enhanced analysis of multi-modal physiological data
- **Active health interventions**: Closed-loop systems for personalized cardiovascular health management
- **Clinical validation**: Real-world evidence generation for wearable device clinical utility
